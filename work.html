Construction

We received the land for the construction work for setup of the organization from Pashupati Area Development Trust. Afterwards,

  • With the aid of Regional Directorate of Livestock Services Central Region, a shed was build. Also, with the help of the then member of parliament Kanta Bhattarai, store for manure was built.

  • With the help from Samjhana Acharya, the bricks were paved.

  • District Livestock Service Office provided the help in damp proofing of the site after the bricks were paved.

  • For the construction of shed for local cows, Krishna Prem Sanstha provided the help.

  • With the help provided by the animal lovers and cow-lovers, the infrastructure of this centre are built.
