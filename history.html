History

Having majority of Hindu followers, cow is respected and worshipped all over the Nepal. In recent years, the number of aged, disabled and sick cows along with calves are forced to live in the populated street depending on undegradable plastic and household wastes as food.

Unable to bear this situation, our organization was established in 2071 B.S. to improve the living of those helpless victims. Pashupati Area Development Trust have provided 9 ropani of land to establish this organization in Pashupati Parishar,Bankali. With the capital investment from our founder member, home for both cattle and caretakers were constructed. Later on, with the help of various doners, the facility provided to caretakers have increased and more sanitory shelter are constructed for cattle with separate dining pit, hygenic granary and separate shelter for pregnant cows and calve are constructed with regular cleanliness of manure. Recently, with the aim of broadening this rescue operation, a branch is established in Tulsipur, Dang.

Our upcoming project is to establish a larger home for helpless cattle and for which land is already purchased in Gurkot VDC, Kavrepalanchowk. More branches are to be established in various other parts.

Our team is working hard on daily basis to provide sanitation and medical care to the cattle. Providing nutritious food, sanitation on shelter and medical care is our main concern.

Till now, this organization have given family, food along with shelter to 180 cattle altogether. Be a part of our organization and help us reduce their number in the streets to null.
