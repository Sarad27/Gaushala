Food Supply

The centre has managed the food supply, till this date, only because of the helping hands of various doners and animal lovers. We receive food items such as hay, corn, fresh vegetables, fruits, grains and more on regular basis in large quantities. A separate granary is maintained to store food in stock so that they can be supplied to the cattle in fixed and regular interval of time.

We have managed a small van with the help of a doner with the help of which we are able to feed fresh fruits and vegetables to the cattle daily. Also, visitors bring some quantity of food which will be given to the cattle immediately. You can feed the cattle yourself too.
