Rescue Operation

मठ मन्दिर गाईबाछा बचाउ तथा समाज बिकास अभियान has been rescuing abandoned street cattles providing shelter and food. We provide medication to the injured and helpless ones suffering in the crowded city. We have land with shed where hardly 200 cattles can shelter, so we are able to provide help to only 200 helpless lives and we are increasing our help to more lives after land and shed enlargement for which we need a helping hand with helpful spirit.

In our rescue operation, we receive information about the situtaion of homeless cows roaming in the street helplessly in seek of food and love. Then our team of 4-5 rescue members go to that specified location renting a vehicle to transport the cattle. Our rescue team also includes a veterinarian to provide immediate medication in the spot. Then after basic medication, the cattle is loaded in the vehicle and brought to the organization. Afterwards, we ensure quality food, shelter and love to them. If the cattle is a baby calf then we hire a motorcycle and transport the calf in motorcycle.

Due to lack of vehicle owned by the organization, we are compelled to rent a vehicle everytime we have a rescue operation. We have somehow managed to pay for the rented vehicle till now. So, a help for buying a vehicle for the organization is expected.

If you see a homeless and injured cattle in the streets then be sure to notify us. Also, if you suspect any violation in animal rights, be sure to inform us.
